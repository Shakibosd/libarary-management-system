Welcome To My | Library Management Project

A school library is a house or room in the school where books are kept for use by the students and teachers. A library plays a crucial role in the realm of knowledge. A school library is part and parcel of a school. No school is complete without a library. Like many other institutions, we have a library in our school. It is situated on the upstairs of the school building. It is a spacious room having a good number of almirahs containing books on literature, history, geography, science, religion, etc. The books are arranged on different bookshelves according to subjects.
